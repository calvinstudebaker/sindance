Sindance Film Festival 2022

Now Accepting Submissions

Requirements

  1. All attendees must submit a film. Collaboration is strongly encouraged.
  2. All in person attendees must show proof of vaccination including a booster. Boosters are free at Walmart, Kmart, Hmart, pretty much any mart just google it.
  3. Your film must not exceed 5 minutes in length (feel free to make it much shorter)
  4. Due to a longstanding exclusivity agreement with the Lerch Foundation, your film must credit Aileen Lerch as your film’s Goat Handler in the credits. You may include “No Goats Were Handled in the Making of This Film” in the rare case that statement applies.
  5. Submit your film by 5pm Jan 22, 2022
