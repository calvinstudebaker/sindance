Requirements

  1. All attendees must submit a film. Collaboration is strongly encouraged.
  2. Your film must not exceed 3 minutes in length (feel free to make it much shorter)
  3. Due to a longstanding exclusivity agreement with the Lerch Foundation, your film must credit Aileen Lerch as your film's Goat Handler in the credits. You may include "No Goats Were Handled in the Making of This Film" in the rare case that statement applies.
  4. Submit your film by 5pm Friday March 28, 2025
