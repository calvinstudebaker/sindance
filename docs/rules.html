Sindance Film Festival

Now Accepting Submissions

Submit Your Film To submissions@sindancefilmfestival.com

Requirements

  1. All attendees must submit a film. Collaboration is strongly encouraged.
  2. Your film must not exceed 2 minutes in length (feel free to make it much shorter)
  3. Due to a longstanding exclusivity agreement with the Lerch Foundation and PETA, your film must credit Aileen Lerch as your film’s Goat Handler in the credits. Please include “No Goats Were Handled in the Making of This Film” if applicable.
  4. Submit your film to submissions@sindancefilmfestival.com by 5pm May 9, 2020
