Sindistdance Film Festival 2021

Now Accepting Submissions

Requirements

  1. All attendees must submit a film. Collaboration is strongly encouraged.
  2. Your film must not exceed 3 minutes in length (feel free to make it much shorter)
  3. Due to a longstanding exclusivity agreement with the Lerch Foundation, your film must credit Aileen Lerch as your film’s Goat Handler in the credits. Please include “No Goats Were Handled in the Making of This Film” if applicable.
  4. Submit your film by 5pm March 27, 2021
